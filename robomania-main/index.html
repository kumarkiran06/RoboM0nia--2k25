ABOUT US

Welcome to Robomania-2K25:Learn. innovate.Excel

Robomania, the annual technical fest of PMEC Berhampur, is organized in the month of March spanning over three days. Commenced with a motto of providing technocrats all over the country, a platform to showcase their technical skills and talent, Robomania has come a long way over the years since its inception.It is one of the eminent fests in the eastern region of the Indian subcontinent today which has made an impact on several college students across the country.
